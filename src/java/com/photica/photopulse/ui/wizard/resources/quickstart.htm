PhotoPulseTM Quickstart
Photica PhotoPulseTM software makes it easy to create dynamic, interactive slideshows using your own photos and music - in three simple steps.
Choose your photos
Click the Photos... button and choose the photos to include in your show. Control-click to select multiple files.
Choose your music
If you want to include music in your show, click the Music... button and choose an MP3 audio file. You do not have to use music in your show.
Create your show
Click the Create... button and choose where you want to save your show. You can choose to save your show as a Macromedia FlashTM file in a web page, or as a standalone Windows® application (EXE).
